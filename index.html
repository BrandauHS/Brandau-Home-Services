Our Story

Full-Service, For Every Kind Of Home

Brandau Home Services started with a simple idea: homeowners, landlords, realtors, and community organizations across Chicagoland all need the same thing — a team that actually shows up, communicates clearly, and does the job right the first time.

At the core of everything we do is a belief in being honest and doing right by people — even when it's the harder path. That's the standard we hold ourselves to, and it's how we aim to become your trusted home service company for the long haul. Every day we're working to better ourselves and build systems that make your experience smoother, clearer, and more dependable — because when we get better, you're the one who feels it.

We don't take any of it for granted. We're genuinely thankful for every customer who's given us the chance to earn their trust, and that gratitude is exactly what pushes us to keep raising the bar.

It's also why we created Brandau Home Care — because we believe a safe, well-kept home shouldn't come down to what you can afford. Home Care exists to lend a hand to neighbors who need it most, so no one gets left behind. We want to be a part of this community, not apart from it — showing up for the people in it, on the job and beyond.

One point of contact you can rely on. Interior to exterior — we're here to help, and here to stay.
