The Materials Exchange Program

Good Materials Shouldn't Go To Waste

What's extra for you could be everything for someone else.

Chances are your last project left something behind — good stuff sitting in a garage, closet, or warehouse with nowhere to go. Doesn't matter how much you've got or how you got it: one extra door or a whole warehouse of leftovers, it can all go toward something that matters — a safer, better home for someone who needs it.

Every donation, big or small, gets treated the same way and goes toward the same thing: making someone's home safer and better.

Appliances

Old stove, fridge, washer, or dryer still running fine but not needed anymore? We'll find it a home with someone who needs it more than the dump does.

Exterior Doors & Door Hardware

A solid exterior door or the hardware to go with it can be the difference between a home that's secure and one that isn't — new or leftover, it's exactly what we're looking for.

Faucets & Plumbing Fixtures

New faucets, plumbing fixtures, or extra plumbing parts sitting in a box can turn into a fully working bathroom or kitchen for someone who needs it.

Electrical Fixtures & Parts

New electrical fixtures, plus extra electrical parts and hardware, are often exactly what's standing between a family and a safe, working home.

Safety & Accessibility Items

Grab bars, ramps, handrails, and other safety or accessibility items can turn a dangerous home into a safe one for someone who needs it most.

Raw Building Materials

Leftover lumber, trim, drywall, and other raw building materials don't have to sit in a garage — they can frame up someone else's repair.

Our Promise To You

Anything you donate to us gets used in good faith toward someone who actually needs it — no games, no sitting around collecting dust. We work through donations first come, first served, and fit these jobs into our normal work week alongside our paying jobs. It might not happen overnight, but it will happen.

We're not a registered nonprofit — just a local company that feels a real responsibility to take care of our community. If you've got materials to give a second life, partner with us and let's put them to good use.

Why We Do This

Giving Back To The Community That Built Us

Brandau Home Services was built on showing up for people across Chicagoland — and that doesn't stop at paying jobs. Home repairs can get expensive fast, and for a lot of families, a broken step, a leaking roof, or a door that won't lock isn't just an inconvenience — it's a real safety risk they can't afford to fix on their own.

We want to be the contact that community organizations, nonprofits, and churches can call when someone in their community needs help with home repairs. Whether that means a sponsored repair, a reduced rate, or simply showing up quickly when it matters, we're here to help where we can.
