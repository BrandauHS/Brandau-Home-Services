What It Is

What Is Brandau Neighbor Care?

Our way of making sure a repair someone can’t afford never turns into a danger they have to live with.

Brandau Neighbor Care is the give-back program of Brandau Home Services. We provide essential home repairs — the leaks, the unsafe wiring, the broken locks, the drafty doors — for Chicagoland families and individuals who genuinely can’t afford them. Using rescued materials and honest work, and partnering with the churches, nonprofits, and case managers who know who needs help, we keep good people in homes that are safe, warm, and secure.

Who it’s for

Chicagoland families and individuals who can’t afford essential repairs — the elderly, the homebound, and anyone a broken home has put at risk.

What we do

Real repairs that keep a home livable: roofs, wiring, water, locks, doors, accessibility, and the small fixes that matter most.

How it works

We use rescued materials and our own labor — often free or heavily sponsored — and lean on community partners to reach the people who need us.

Why We Do This

Giving Back To The Community That Built Us

Brandau Home Services was built on showing up for people across Chicagoland — and that doesn't stop at paying jobs. Home repairs can get expensive fast, and for a lot of families, a broken step, a leaking roof, or a door that won't lock isn't just an inconvenience — it's a real safety risk they can't afford to fix on their own.

We want to be the contact that community organizations, nonprofits, and churches can call when someone in their community needs help with home repairs. Whether that means a sponsored repair, a reduced rate, or simply showing up quickly when it matters, we're here to help where we can.

Who We Partner With

A Resource For Your Organization

If your organization works with people who need home repair help, we want to hear from you. There are two ways we partner: we help the individuals and families you refer — often through donated materials and our own time, at little or no cost to them — and for your organization’s own repair and rehab projects, we offer a standing discounted rate. We can’t do every job for free, but we’ll always make the mission work.

Community Organizations

A repair partner for the neighbors, families, and individuals your organization already works with.

Nonprofits & Charities

An extra set of hands for the home repair side of the families and individuals your programs already serve.

Churches

A trusted contact for repair needs among your congregation, especially for elderly and homebound members.

Case Managers & Social Workers

A streamlined referral process and fast turnaround for urgent needs like heat, water, or safety hazards.

How It Works

How Brandau Neighbor Care Works

1. Reach Out
