What It Is

What Is Brandau Neighbor Care?

Our way of making sure a repair someone can’t afford never turns into a danger they have to live with.

Brandau Neighbor Care is the give-back program of Brandau Home Services. We provide essential home repairs — leaks, unsafe wiring, broken locks, drafty doors — for Chicagoland families and individuals who genuinely can’t afford them, using rescued materials and honest work. We partner with the churches, nonprofits, and case managers who know who needs help most.

Who it’s for

Chicagoland families and individuals who can’t afford essential repairs — the elderly, the homebound, and anyone a broken home has put at risk.

What we do

Real repairs that keep a home livable: roof leaks, wiring, water, locks, doors, accessibility, and the small fixes that matter most.

How it works

Rescued materials and our own labor — often free or heavily sponsored — with community partners helping us reach the people who need us.

How It Works

How Brandau Neighbor Care Works

Whether you need a hand yourself or you’re a church, nonprofit, or case manager referring a neighbor, the path is the same.

1. Reach Out

Call, text, or send a message — for yourself, or on behalf of a neighbor who needs help.

2. We Assess The Need

We talk through the situation and figure out the right kind of help for the timeline.

3. We Get It Done

We schedule and complete the work with the same care and quality as any other job.

For partner organizations: we help the people you refer at little or no cost, and offer a standing discounted rate on your own repair and rehab projects. We can’t do every job for free, but we’ll always make the mission work.
