Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Brandau Home Services (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you visit brandauhomeservices.com (the “Site”) or contact us through it. We keep this simple: we only collect what we need to respond to you and to understand how the Site is used.

Information you give us

When you fill out a quote or contact form, you may provide your name, phone number, email address, ZIP code, and a description of the work you need. This is sent to us through a third-party form-delivery provider and delivered to our email so we can get back to you. We use it only to respond to your request and provide our services.

The Snap Diagnosis photo tool

If you use our Snap Diagnosis tool, the photo you upload is sent to an automated image-analysis service to generate an instant read of what’s in the image. The photo is used only for that analysis and is not stored on our servers. Please avoid including people, license plates, documents, or other sensitive details in photos you upload.

The address / service-area checker

If you type an address into our service-area checker, that address is sent to a third-party mapping service to place it on the map and measure the distance to our service area. We do not send your typed address to our analytics or advertising tools — only coarse, non-identifying information such as whether the location was in range and the approximate distance.

Information collected automatically (analytics & cookies)

With your consent, we use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use the Site — for example, which pages are viewed, how people arrive, and which actions (like requesting a quote or clicking to call) are taken. This may involve cookies and information such as your approximate location, device, and browser.

  • Analytics run only after you accept the cookie banner. We use Google Consent Mode, which defaults everything to “denied” until you choose.
  • If you decline, no analytics cookies are set and no analytics data is sent to Google.
  • We do not put personal information (your name, address, email, or phone number) into any analytics event.
  • We may use this data, in aggregate, to measure and improve our advertising. We do not sell your personal information.

Service providers

To run the Site, we rely on a small number of trusted third-party service providers — for example, for website analytics, contact-form delivery, hosting, mapping, and automated photo analysis. Each provider receives only the information it needs to perform its function and handles that information under its own privacy policy. For website analytics we use Google Analytics, as described above.

How we use information

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide quotes and services.
  • To operate, secure, and improve the Site.
  • To understand which content and services are most useful (analytics).

Your choices

You can decline analytics cookies using the banner or the button above. You can ask us to access or delete the information you’ve submitted to us by emailing office@brandauhomeservices.com. Most browsers also let you block or delete cookies in their settings.

Children’s privacy

The Site is intended for adults and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we’ll change the “Last updated” date above.
