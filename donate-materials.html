Donate Materials

What's extra for you could be everything for someone else.

Tell us what you've got and where it is. We'll add it to our list of available materials and reach out to coordinate next steps — whether that's picking it up, helping you get it to an organization, or holding onto it until someone needs it.

Materials Donation Form

How It Works

We'll Take It From Here

Once you submit the form, your donation gets added to our internal list of available materials. If you're able to hold onto the items for a while, we'll keep them on file as a resource and reach out as soon as we — or a family or organization we work with — has a need for them.

If you'd rather not store them, let us know and we'll coordinate getting them to us or directly to an organization, so they don't end up sitting around or in a landfill.
